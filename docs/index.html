We make sure that no one remains  underprivileged no more. and for that we need your help, not necessarily the donation but your word of mouth.

Our Mission

We have been on the front lines of humanitarian crises, medical emergencies, and natural catastrophes since EK SAHYOG was founded in the wake of COVID 19. Despite the size of the crisis, we take on the task, rethink what is possible, and act to support hundreds of those in need in surviving and thriving. Through devoted collaborations and a desire for innovation, our on-the-ground experience has expanded to several regions.
